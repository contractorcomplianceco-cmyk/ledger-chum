# Legal & Accounting Review Register

Live view: `/feature-registry/legal-accounting`

Every feature carrying `requires_legal_review`, `requires_accountant_review`,
or `requires_tax_review` appears here with an owner and reviewer.

## Highest-risk clusters

- **Profit Sharing & Ownership Participation** — legal + accounting + tax, high financial risk, owner approval.
- **Owner Finance** — accountant + tax + owner approval; mixed-use rules require accountant sign-off.
- **Investor Distributions & Strategic Partner Agreements** — legal + tax.
- **Dormant Pass-Through / Escheatment** — legal + tax + accounting; state-by-state escheatment rules.
- **Commission Types** — legal (contracts) + accounting (recognition timing).
- **Multi-Entity Accounting** — accounting (eliminations) + legal (intercompany) + tax (allocations).
- **Legal, Tax & Professional Services** — privileged documents, matter numbers, sensitive access.
